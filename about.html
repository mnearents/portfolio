About Me

Hi, I am a UX/Product designer from Huntington Beach. I am passionate about crafting empowering experiences for mobile, web, or beyond. I am an entrepreneur at heart and crave ideation and invention.

I began college as a pre-med student. It wasn't for me, so I fell back on my Psychology major. I took some computer programming courses and an HCI course and realized I was destined to be a UX designer. I never felt like a creative person growing up, but UX unlocked creative pathways in my brain.

Neither of my parents graduated college, so I was determined to not only go to graduate school, but to get my PhD. However, I soon realized I wanted the PhD title for the wrong reasons. I reassessed my goals, took an awesome UX position close to family and never looked back. I've been in the industry ever since.

In my spare time I hang with the family, ride dirt bikes, restore cars, dream about startup ideas, code apps, and write music. Interesting fact: in a previous life I was a casino card dealer. I delt six different card games including BlackJack. I learned to love math and to perform under pressure (try remembering card totals for a full table of eight players and calculating a blackjack at 1.5 times on a $37.50 bet while your entire table watches you in silence).
