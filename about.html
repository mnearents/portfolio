About Me

Hi, I am a UX/Product designer from Huntington Beach. I am passionate about crafting empowering experiences, whether it be via mobile, web, or beyond. I am an entrepreneur at heart and crave ideation and invention.

I started my college career as a pre-med student majoring in Psychology. Pre-med wasn't for me, so I fell back on my degree. Once I found I/O and Human Factors, I knew a career in applied psychology was what I wanted. My first HCI course lit a fire inside me and I found my calling. I never felt like a creative person growing up, but UX permanently unlocked pathways of creativity in my brain.

Neither of my parents graduated college, so I was determined to not only go to graduate school, but to get my PhD. However, I soon realized I was attending for the wrong reasons, and when an awesome job opportunity presented itself, I took it and never looked back. I've been in the industry ever since.
