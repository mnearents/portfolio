UX Design

Pool Chemistry App

Mobile Design

Background

I designed an end-to-end experience for pool owners to manage their pool chemistry. The ultimate goal was to help people get more enjoyment out of their pools by reducing the effort and cognition required to balance water.

This project is currently under NDA. Sorry, no screen shots or specific details.

UX Research

We used various research methods to inform our designs such as subject matter experts, on-site store visits, and competitive analysis. Unfortunately with a short deadline, we had to postpone user interviews for a later time.

A team from Cie Digital Labs visited the client's location and gathered information around customer pain points from store clerks. These visits were video recorded for me to utilize in design. Several pain points were identified.

I also looked at some competitors such as Clorox and Home Depot's pool app. We assessed their UI structure, user flows, and aesthetics.

I spoke to subject matter experts in order to inform the information architecture and layout hierarchy.

UX Design

I used Axure to create flow diagrams for every major user flow, as well as wire-frames for ever screen. I included notes for developers and visual designers. Overall the client is very happy with our work. We are approaching launch and I hope to evaluate analytics, as well as conduct user-testing to fix any minor issues. I also hope to conduct interviews to inform/prioritize post-MVP features.
