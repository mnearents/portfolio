Beautiful branding

Branding, Web Design
Seconds / 2016

I'm beyond thrilled to finally unveil the new branding I've been working so hard on behind the scenes for this brand! When cluent first contacted me I resonated so strongly with her story and our first call proved to me right away that we'd be a great fit. So without further ado, I'm so excited to share a peek into the process behind brand new look!

Target audience is women who are passionate about achieving their dreams, building a successful online business, supporting other small businesses, and creating a life they love around their small children. They need a beautiful online presence, but are intimidated by the tech side of things and are limited the cost of high quality website development. Brand identity appeals to aspiring female business owners and seasoned ladypreneurs alike. Its refined and feminine style reflects the quality, passion, and professionalism.
