UX Design

Petco

Mobile Design, Ecommerce

Background

I was responsible for leading the user experience design of Petco's ecommerce mobile app. I wire-framed end-to-end experiences for shopping, account management, Petco's repeat delivery (subscription), and store locator.

UX Research

I looked at one direct competitor, Chewy, and several indirect competitors such asÂ Walmart, Amazon, Target, Etsy, and Home Depot. I worked closely with Petco's subject matter experts to inform my designs.

UX Design

Our main goal was to help automate pet care. Petco has Repeat Delivery which is a subscription for pet supplies. To highlight the benefits of Repeat Delivery to the customer, we provided the shipping option on the product page, as a pre-cursor to adding to cart.

Another focus was to make the app pet-centric. We created a pet profile to personalize the shopping experience. Many of the personalization features were cut from the MVP due to Petco's legacy systems.

Visual Design

I worked closely with visual designers to translate the wire frames into high-fidelity mockups.
